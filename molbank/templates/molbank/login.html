Traditional boundaries of scientific disciplines are rapidly disappearing, chemists, physicists, biologists and physicians are coming together in developing platform nano-biotechnolgy for combating dreaded diseases, in exploring solar energy as alternative energy source, in designing various strategic materials for example.
